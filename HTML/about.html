My Top 9

A new, centralized, social website bringing you even closer to your absolute top besties. Our website allows you to keep in close contact with 9 of your closest friends or family members, without the clutter of the "others".

Our Story

Nowadays, there are so many different ways to connect with the world. But, do you ever feel like you're missing important moments or events that have been shared by those you care about the most? My Top 9 wipes away the clutter and the need to scroll aimlessly through your feed, so that you don't have to see your ex's grandmother's best friend's trip to Spain ever again.

We filter out the posts you actually want to see without having to hit that awkward "unfollow" button. You'll be kept in the loop with your best friends, and feel like you haven't missed a beat. Even when you're thousands of miles away.

Features

Through the exclusive sharing of photos, videos, chats, and a My Top 9 Friend Location Map, you're never too far away from your main crew.

  • Share photos
  • Chat
  • Post a status
  • Categorize/Organize social circles
  • Locate friends
  • Stay in touch

Users

My Top 9 users have the opportunity to control their ultimate social experience by keeping ties tight with their closest friends, relatives, and peers. Users will have the ability to connect instantly with their Top 9, by categorizing their favorites. Users can organize and create groups for best friends, family members, and co-workers, just to name a few. You will have access to the features by the click of a button, which you may scroll through the menu and customize your page to show exactly what you want, and how you want it.
